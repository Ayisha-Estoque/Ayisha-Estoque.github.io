About

Established in 2012, it’s the first world-class science museum in the Philippines, designed to inspire a passion for science among visitors of all ages.

Focus

Science and natural history

Location

J.Y. Campos Park, 3rd Avenue, Bonifacio Global City, Taguig, 1634 Metro Manila, Philippines

The Mind Museum

The Mind Museum is the Philippines’ premier interactive science museum, offering a space where visitors can explore the wonders of science and nature. Its cutting-edge exhibits make it a family-friendly destination for learning about biology, physics, earth science, and technology through hands-on experiences.
