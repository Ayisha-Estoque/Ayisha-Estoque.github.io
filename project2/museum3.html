Location

Kaisa-Angelo King Heritage Center, Anda Street, Intramuros, Manila, 1002 Metro Manila, Philippines

Bahay Tsinoy (The Filipino-Chinese Museum)

Situated in the historic Intramuros district, Bahay Tsinoy celebrates the deep historical ties between the Filipino and Chinese communities. The museum traces the Chinese influence on the Philippines through migration, commerce, and cultural integration, showcasing centuries of collaboration between these two cultures.
