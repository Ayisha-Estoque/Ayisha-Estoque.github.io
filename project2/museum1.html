Museum Facts: Location, Focus & Background

About

Established in 1901, the museum was originally founded as the Insular Museum of Ethnology and has since expanded into the foremost institution for preserving Philippine art, culture, and heritage.

Focus

Filipino heritage, history, culture, and arts.

Location

P. Burgos Drive, Rizal Park, Ermita, Manila, 1000 Metro Manila, Philippines.

National Museum of the Philippines

The National Museum of the Philippines serves as the guardian of the country's cultural heritage. It plays a pivotal role in preserving and showcasing the rich historical, artistic, and natural wealth of the nation through its various divisions. It’s a place where the country’s history is brought to life through art, archaeology, anthropology, and biodiversity.
